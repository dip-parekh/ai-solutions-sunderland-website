Our Story

Founded in 2018 in Sunderland, AI-Solutions began with a simple mission: to make technology work better for people.

Our founders, all experienced in the tech industry, noticed a common problem across organizations of all sizes. Despite significant investments in digital workplace tools, employees often struggled with complex interfaces, fragmented systems, and inefficient processes.

Our Mission & Values

At AI-Solutions, we're driven by a clear purpose and guided by strong values in everything we do.

Our Mission

To transform digital employee experiences through intelligent, adaptive AI solutions that make technology more intuitive, efficient, and enjoyable to use.

Our Vision

Human-First Innovation
